🏫 Export for Google Classroom
Post this WebQuest as a Classroom assignment in two steps — the instructions go in the assignment description, and the full content goes into a linked Google Doc.
1

In Google Classroom, click Create → Assignment. Paste the assignment description below into the Instructions field.

2

Open a new Google Doc. Paste the full WebQuest content below into the Doc. Attach it to the assignment using Add → Google Drive.

3

Set due date, assign to the right class, and click Assign.
